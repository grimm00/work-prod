## ğŸ“ Research Organization

### Research Document Structure

Each research document includes:

1. **Research Questions** - What we're investigating
2. **Methodology** - How we researched
3. **Findings** - What we discovered
4. **Analysis** - What it means
5. **Recommendations** - What we should do
6. **Decision** - What we decided (if finalized)
7. **References** - Sources and resources

### From Research to Decisions

**Flow:**
```
Research Document â†’ Analysis â†’ Recommendation â†’ ADR (if architectural)
```

- **Research documents** capture investigation process
- **ADRs** (Architecture Decision Records) capture final architectural decisions
- Research documents can reference multiple ADRs
- ADRs always reference supporting research

- Review official documentation
